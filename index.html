📌 Important Tips
⏱️Rest as long as you need between sets — around 3–4 minutes is the sweet spot. Never rush. Always feel fresh before the next set.
🪶Always start light. You should never reach failure — leave 3–4 reps in the tank each set, especially in the first weeks.
📈Progressive overload: try to add a little weight or 1–2 reps every week once you feel comfortable and ready.
🎯First 2–3 weeks are all about learning the movements. Watch the videos, go slow, feel the target muscle working.
🥩Protein: aim for ~1.6g per kg of bodyweight daily. It makes a real difference for recovery and progress.
😊Most importantly — enjoy it! The best workout is the one you actually look forward to.
